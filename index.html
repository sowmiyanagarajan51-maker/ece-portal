Student Record Management

Welcome to the official ECE Student Portal. This platform allows students to autonomously maintain their academic profiles, project submissions, and core details, significantly reducing administrative overhead for faculty management.

How it works

Self-Registration

Students input their contact details, roll numbers, and academic milestones directly.

Progress Tracking

Allows seamless updates of semester GPAs, mini-projects, and elective choices.

Centralized Admin Control

The Admin dashboard allows the Professor to filter, sort, export, and review all student records instantly without typing data manually.

Student Instructions

1. Create Account

Click on 'Sign Up' in the top right. Use your official University Registration/Roll Number.

2. Complete Profile

Provide accurate core identification data, address points, and contact information details.

3. Maintain Data

Log back in at the end of each semester to keep your record accurate and up to date.
